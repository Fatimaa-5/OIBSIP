● BIOGRAPHY

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. From a young age, he displayed an extraordinary curiosity about the world around him.

He developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics. His famous equation E = mc² is one of the most recognizable formulas in the world.

Beyond physics, Einstein was a humanitarian who advocated for peace, civil rights, and education. He believed in the power of imagination and knowledge to build a better future.

Einstein's legacy continues to inspire scientists, thinkers, and dreamers across generations.
